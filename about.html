Tollywood Heroes

Ram Charan

Ram Charan is a prominent actor, producer, and entrepreneur in the Telugu film industry. Born on March 27, 1985, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, Ram Charan is the son of veteran actor Chiranjeevi. He made his acting debut with the film "Chirutha" and quickly rose to fame with his remarkable performances and charismatic persona. Ram Charan is known for his dedication to his craft and his ability to deliver blockbuster hits consistently.

Mahesh Babu

Mahesh Babu, often referred to as the "Prince of Tollywood," is one of the most celebrated actors in the Telugu film industry. Born on August 9, 1975, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, Mahesh Babu made his acting debut as a child artist and later rose to prominence with his remarkable performances in various Telugu films. He is known for his versatility, charm, and impeccable acting skills, which have earned him a massive fan following across the globe.

NTR

Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao Jr., popularly known as Junior NTR or Tarak, is a versatile actor in Telugu cinema. Born on May 20, 1983, in Hyderabad, Telangana, India, NTR comes from a prestigious film family with a legacy spanning generations. He is known for his intense performances, exceptional dialogue delivery, and charismatic screen presence. Junior NTR has carved a niche for himself in the industry with his diverse roles and acting prowess.

Allu Arjun

Allu Arjun, fondly called the "Stylish Star," is one of the leading actors in Telugu cinema. Born on April 8, 1983, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, Allu Arjun comes from a prestigious film family. He gained recognition for his unique dance style, energy-packed performances, and ability to portray a wide range of characters with ease. Allu Arjun's popularity extends beyond the Telugu-speaking states, making him a well-known name in the Indian film industry.

Pawan Kalyan

Pawan Kalyan, also known as Power Star, is a prominent figure in the Telugu film industry. Born on September 2, 1971, in Bapatla, Andhra Pradesh, India, Pawan Kalyan is not only an actor but also a filmmaker, screenwriter, and politician. He garnered immense popularity for his dynamic on-screen presence, powerful dialog delivery, and charismatic personality. Apart from his contributions to cinema, Pawan Kalyan is known for his involvement in social and political activities.

Prabhas

Prabhas, best known for his role as Baahubali, is a highly acclaimed actor in the Indian film industry. Born on October 23, 1979, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, Prabhas gained nationwide recognition and international fame for his portrayal of the titular character in the Baahubali film series. His dedication, commitment, and hard work in bringing such iconic characters to life have earned him immense admiration from audiences and critics alike.

Medium Range Heroes

Nani

Nani, whose real name is Naveen Babu Ghanta, is a popular actor in Telugu cinema. He made his debut with the film "Ashta Chamma" and gained recognition for his natural acting skills. Nani is known for his versatility and has delivered several critically acclaimed performances in films like "Eega," "Bhale Bhale Magadivoy," and "Jersey." Apart from acting, Nani has also worked as an assistant director and producer.

Vijay Devarakonda

Vijay Devarakonda is best known for his role in the film "Arjun Reddy," which earned him widespread acclaim. He has quickly risen to fame with his unconventional choices of roles and intense performances. Vijay is recognized for his charismatic screen presence and is considered one of the most promising actors in the Telugu film industry. Some of his other notable films include "Geetha Govindam" and "Dear Comrade."

Ram Pothineni

Ram Pothineni, commonly referred to as Energetic Star, is a Telugu actor known for his energetic performances and youthful charm. He made his debut with the film "Devadasu" and has since appeared in numerous successful films. Ram is known for his versatility in portraying different roles, ranging from romantic to action-packed characters. Some of his popular films include "Ready," "Nenu Sailaja," and "Hello Guru Prema Kosame."

Vishwaksen

Vishwaksen Naidu is an Indian actor and director primarily working in Telugu cinema. He gained recognition for his performance in the independent film "Falaknuma Das," which he also directed. Known for his unconventional roles and bold choices, Vishwaksen has carved a niche for himself in the industry. Apart from acting, he has also ventured into filmmaking and has garnered praise for his directorial debut.

Rana Dagguabati

Rana Daggubati is an Indian actor, producer, and entrepreneur known for his work in Telugu, Tamil, and Hindi cinema. He gained widespread acclaim for his portrayal of the antagonist Bhallaladeva in the "Baahubali" series. Rana has showcased his versatility by taking on diverse roles in films like "Leader," "Ghazi," and "Nene Raju Nene Mantri." Apart from acting, he is actively involved in film production and various other business ventures.

Sharwanand

Sharwanand Myneni is a Telugu actor known for his work in both mainstream and parallel cinema. He made his debut with the film "5va Tareeku" and has since appeared in numerous commercially successful films. Sharwanand is known for his subtle yet impactful performances and has garnered critical acclaim for his roles. Some of his notable films include "Prasthanam," "Run Raja Run," and "Mahanubhavudu."

Naga Chaitanya

Naga Chaitanya Akkineni is an Indian actor who primarily works in Telugu cinema. He belongs to the prestigious Akkineni family and made his debut with the film "Josh." Naga Chaitanya has showcased his acting prowess in films like "Ye Maaya Chesave," "Manam," and "Premam." He is known for his charming screen presence and has established himself as a bankable actor in the industry.

Nikhil

Nikhil Siddhartha is an Indian actor known for his work predominantly in Telugu cinema. He made his acting debut with the film "Happy Days" and gained recognition for his performance. Nikhil is known for his versatility and has portrayed a variety of roles across different genres. Some of his notable films include "Swamy Ra Ra," "Karthikeya," and "Ekkadiki Pothavu Chinnavada."

Nithin

Nithin Kumar Reddy, known mononymously as Nithin, is a Telugu actor and producer. He made his debut with the film "Jayam" and has since appeared in numerous successful films. Nithin is known for his boy-next-door charm and has garnered a significant fan following. Some of his popular films include "Ishq," "Gunde Jaari Gallanthayyinde," and "A Aa."

Sai Dharam Tej

Sai Dharam Tej is an Indian actor who primarily works in Telugu cinema. He belongs to the prestigious Chiranjeevi family and made his debut with the film "Pilla Nuvvu Leni Jeevitham." Sai Dharam Tej is known for his energetic performances and has established himself as a promising actor in the industry. Some of his notable films include "Subramanyam for Sale," "Supreme," and "Chitralahari."
